About Me

I'm Oliver, the creative mind behind RawuVisuals, a creative video agency and personal portfolio where I unite storytelling, production, and aesthetics. I'm educated as a producer from Multiplatform Storytelling & Production, a film education that combines visual storytelling, project management, and sustainable film production.

I work with everything from commercial films and content strategies to events and short films, and thrive best in between the visual and organizational part of filmmaking. Whether I'm behind the camera or editing a project, my work is about creating content that feels authentic and conveys a clear message that reaches the target audience effectively.

For me, each project is a collaboration where quality and trust go hand in hand. I believe in transparency, creative freedom, and a work process built on respect for both the story and collaboration partners.
