Hård Ting - Music Video

The Video

A self-initiated collaboration from my time at MSP. Hård Ting is a retro and humorous music video for the Danish rap duo of the same name.

My Role

Director of Photography, Producer, Editor and Coordinator, responsible for both the creative vision and production process.

Visual Style

Inspired by 80s aesthetics and old school hip hop, the video mixes humor with stylized cinematography and color driven storytelling.

Production

Shot with a small dedicated crew where I had a say in everything from planning to post production, keeping the tone playful and cohesive.

Outcome

The video defined the duo's early visual identity and set the tone for their future releases.
