Project Details

My Role

  • Producer: Project management and production coordination
  • Editor: Post-production and video editing
  • Coordinator: Collaboration between all involved parties

Result

A dynamic music video that captures the essence of Hård Ting's unique style. The project demonstrates my ability to handle multiple roles simultaneously and deliver a cohesive creative product.
