Epic Booking

Project Overview

Since 2024, I have collaborated with Epic Booking as a freelance videographer, producing a series of aftermovies for major folk high school events across Denmark. The partnership has allowed me to capture youth culture, energy, and celebration through fast-paced, cinematic storytelling.

My Role

  • Freelance Videographer: Complete video production from shooting to final edit
  • Event Specialist: Capturing the atmosphere, joy, and connection at each event

Creative Approach

Each aftermovie was produced under tight deadlines, requiring quick decision-making and a clear visual identity. My focus was to translate the intensity and freedom of the parties into immersive visuals that reflected Epic Booking's brand and community spirit.

Production Quality

Working independently, I handled everything from pre-production to final edit, ensuring consistency and high technical standards across multiple events. The collaboration allowed me to refine my workflow for dynamic, low-light shooting and fast turnaround editing.

Impact

The aftermovies became a key part of Epic Booking's marketing, helping shape their visual identity and strengthen their position as a leading event organizer for folk high schools in Denmark.
