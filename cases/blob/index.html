BLOB

Project Overview

BLOB is an independent short film completed in 2026, where I took on the dual role of Visual Producer and Production Manager. The project became a key step in my development as a filmmaker, my first major production working with professional actors and a full-scale crew.

My Role

As Visual Producer, I was responsible for the overall look and creative direction of the film, shaping its tone and visual rhythm in collaboration with the Director, DP & Scriptwriters.

As Production Manager, I handled the logistical backbone of the production, coordinating schedules, budgets, and departments from pre to post-production.

Creative Approach

BLOB blends realism with absurd and experimental elements, exploring how ordinary environments can feel unsettling when pushed to their emotional limits. My role was to bridge the creative and practical sides of production, ensuring that the visual ideas could actually live within the boundaries of time, space, and resources.

Process & Challenges

The film was my first time managing a team of professional actors and crew, which demanded clear structure, communication, and adaptability under pressure. Finding that balance between creative ambition and production reality became one of the most rewarding parts of the process.
