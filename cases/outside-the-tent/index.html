Outside the Tent

About the Project

Short film from 2021 exploring themes like isolation. I worked across multiple roles including Editor, 1st AC, Sound Designer and Boom Operator, gaining experience in both production and post.

Across the Production

From technical camera work to creative sound design, I was involved in the entire process. It gave me insight into how image, sound and pacing come together to tell a cohesive horror story.

Learning by Doing

Outside the Tent became a key learning project that strengthened my technical foundation and deepened my understanding of teamwork and workflow on set.
