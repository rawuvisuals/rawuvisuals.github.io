Toby Tang

About the Project

Creative transmedia campaign developed for Dansk Tang in 2023. I worked as Producer and 1st AD on a playful, cross-platform concept that aimed to connect storytelling and sustainability. The campaign was never released but served as a strong showcase of cross-media production.

My Role

Producer and First Assistant Director, handling coordination, budgeting and on-set management while keeping the creative direction aligned across all formats.

Creative Process

The goal was to build a campaign that worked across film, social and interactive elements while keeping Dansk Tang's identity consistent. I led scheduling, team coordination and ensured each part of the production supported the same tone and narrative.

Outcome

Even though the campaign was not released, the project proved our ability to merge storytelling, structure and brand identity across multiple platforms in one cohesive production.
