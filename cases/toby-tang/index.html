My Role

  • First Assistant Director: Managing on-set operations, scheduling, and ensuring smooth production workflow

Transmedial Production

As Producer and First AD on the Toby Tang campaign for Dansk Tang, I focused on coordinating production elements that work seamlessly across different media platforms. Cross-Platform Coordination

This project allowed me to demonstrate my production management capabilities across multiple media formats, working closely with directors, creative teams, and talent to realize a unified vision through strategic planning and effective on-set leadership.

While the Toby Tang campaign was ultimately never released, the production process demonstrated my ability to produce and coordinate transmedial content, successfully managing the complex logistics required to create engaging material that would have effectively communicated Dansk Tang's brand message across multiple touchpoints and platforms.
