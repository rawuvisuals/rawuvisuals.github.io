Project Details

Social media collaboration from December 2023. Within the organization Oil Team, I functioned as editor for selected YouTube videos and had responsibility for editing and strategic management of their entire TikTok presence.

My Role

Strategic Content Creation

My role as editor involved meticulous curation and editing of selected YouTube content pieces to ensure a cohesive and professional presentation on the platform. Simultaneously, I had overall responsibility for editing and strategy behind Oil Team's TikTok presence.

Platform Management

This included content creation, trend monitoring, and audience interaction. The collaboration demonstrated my abilities in cross-platform editing and strategic approach to managing a company's online image.

Results

During the time I worked as responsible for the company's TikTok, the business grew from 3,000 followers to 22,000 followers in three months - demonstrating the effectiveness of strategic content management and audience engagement.
