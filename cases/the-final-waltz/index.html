The Final Waltz

Project Details

Short film from 2022 where I worked as Director of Photography, Colorgrade and Coordinator. An intimate drama that explored themes of endings and new beginnings.

My Role

  • Director of Photography: Creative responsibility for the film's visual expression and cinematography
  • Colorgrade: Post-production color treatment to create the right mood
  • Coordinator: Ensured smooth communication between cast and crew

Artistic Vision

The project focused on creating a visually beautiful and emotionally engaging narrative through careful cinematography and color treatment. It was a collaboration that required precision in every detail.

Result

A cinematographic short film that demonstrates my ability to combine technical expertise with artistic vision to create meaningful visual storytelling.
