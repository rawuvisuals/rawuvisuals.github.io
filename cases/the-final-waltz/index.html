The Final Waltz

A Story About Endings

A short film from 2022 exploring themes of endings and new beginnings. I worked as Director of Photography and Colorist on this intimate and visually driven story.

Behind the Camera

I was responsible for the film's visual tone and color treatment, shaping the look and atmosphere in close collaboration with the director.

Finding the Right Mood

The focus was on emotion through framing, light and color. Every shot was crafted to match the film's quiet rhythm and to let the visuals carry the story's emotional weight.

The Result on Screen

A visually expressive short film that combines control and sensitivity, using light and tone to reflect change, nostalgia and new beginnings.
