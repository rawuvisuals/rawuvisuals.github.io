Zensorial by BECH

Project Details

Zensorial by BECH is a comprehensive film production project where I played a central role in coordination and logistics. This project demonstrates my experience with both production and sustainability on professional film sets.

My Role

  • Producer: Responsible for project coordination, budget management and production planning
  • Green Manager: Implemented sustainable production methods and environmentally friendly solutions
  • Transport Manager: Coordinated logistics, transport and equipment for all production days

Creative Collaboration

As part of a multidisciplinary production team, I worked closely with directors, cinematographers and the creative team to ensure a smooth production process. My role required both creative understanding and practical problem-solving.

Sustainable Film Production

As Green Manager, I was responsible for implementing environmentally friendly practices on set, including waste management, energy optimization and transport coordination to minimize the project's environmental impact.

Project Coordination

My role as Producer involved coordinating all aspects of production, from pre-production planning to post-production delivery, focusing on meeting schedules and budgets.
