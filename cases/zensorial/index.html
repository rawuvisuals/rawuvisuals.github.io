Zensorial by BECH

Project Overview

Zensorial by BECH is an independent fashion film created in collaboration with Danish designer Anne-Kathrine Bech. The project merges visual storytelling with sustainable production practices, exploring how calm and balance can exist within the noise of modern city life.

My Role

  • Producer: Oversaw project coordination, budget management and production planning
  • Green Manager: Implemented sustainable solutions on set including waste management, transport planning and low-impact catering
  • Transport Manager: Coordinated logistics and ensured smooth operations for all production days

Creative Collaboration

As part of a multidisciplinary team, I worked closely with the director, cinematographer and designer to translate BECH's values into a clear visual identity. Based on her brand values, we developed the collection's tagline "Calm within the Noise", which became the foundation for both the film's mood and visual direction.

Sustainable Production

Sustainability guided every stage of production, from transportation and energy use to catering and waste sorting. As Green Manager, I led our team's effort to minimize environmental impact while maintaining high production quality.

Result

The final film brought BECH's vision to life, translating the brand's focus on calmness and quality into a visual story that connects fashion and sustainability. It strengthened BECH's identity as a conscious menswear label and highlighted how design can inspire a slower, more mindful way of living.
