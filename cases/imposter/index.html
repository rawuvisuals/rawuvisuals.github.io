IMPOSTER - Music Video

Project Details

Music video from 2022 for the band MOEBIUS where I worked as Editor. This project gave me the opportunity to work with rhythmic editing and music video aesthetics.

My Role

  • Editor: Responsible for editing and post-production of the music video

Music Video Aesthetics

IMPOSTER required a precise understanding of the music's rhythm and mood. As editor, it was my responsibility to create a visual narrative that matched the band's energy and style.

Creative Process

Working with MOEBIUS was an educational experience in the music video genre. It required both technical precision and creative intuition to capture the essence of the music visually.

Collaboration

This project strengthened my ability to work closely with musicians and understand their creative vision, while contributing my own cinematic expertise.
