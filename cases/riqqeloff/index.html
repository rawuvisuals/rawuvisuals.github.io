Riqqeloff

About the Project

Since March 2023 I have been editing all long form videos for Finnish creator Riqqeloff. His YouTube channel has around 90,000 subscribers and focuses on gaming content, primarily the game Rust. He is also active on Twitch, where he streams to a growing audience worldwide.

My Role

Video Editor and Creative Collaborator, shaping pacing, flow and visual consistency across his long form videos and helping create a cohesive look for his channel.

Editing Approach

The work involves managing commentary, gameplay footage, transitions and color grading to match Riqqeloff's energetic and humor driven tone. With tight upload schedules, the process is fast, structured and highly collaborative.

So how are we doing?

Consistent editing and creative pacing have contributed to increased viewer engagement and audience growth. So far, on long form content (30 min - 2 hours), we've achieved 10+ million views across our collaboration alone.
